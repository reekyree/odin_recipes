Lasagna

This is my grandmother's famous lasagna recipe. I've always wanted to share it but I was a little self-conscious about it for a long time. Now, the secret is out.

What is the secret, exactly? Parmesan cheese. And asiago cheese. Not just silly mozarella.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Asiago cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ground beef
