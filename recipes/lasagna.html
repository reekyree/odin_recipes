Lasagna

Description

A perfect weeknight lasagna, that tastes just like home. This lasagna starts with a delectible homemade sauce that is incredibly easy to whip up. Along with the sauce, the fresh, full-moisture mozzarella brings a whole new level of flavor to an already flavorful dish.


Ingredients

For the Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef chuck (chuck or round)
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 cups water
    • <1i>(28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 1 bay leaf

For the Lasagna

  • 8 ounces lasagna noodles
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 16 ounces low-fat ricotta cheese
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 16 ounces mozzarella cheese (fresh, whole milk, sliced)

Directions

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Start the sauce about 30 minutes in advance. In a large deep saucepan medium heat, saut√© the onion and garlic in 3 tablespoons of olive oil until onions are soft.
  3. Add the ground beef and brown, stirring frequently.
  4. Add the tomato paste and cook, stirring until bubbling, about 3 minutes. Add water, tomatoes, salt, basil, cayenne pepper, and bay leaf. Stir well.
  5. Reduce heat to low and simmer slowly, uncovered, for 15 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  6. Bring a stockpot full of water to a rolling boil and season with salt. Add the lasagna noodles and cook as the package directs, stirring frequently to prevent sticking. Drain and toss gently with 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
  7. Preheat the oven to 350 F and grab a large 3-quart rectangular baking dish.
  8. Spoon 4 to 6 tablespoons of the sauce or just enough to cover the bottom of the baking dish.
  9. Top with a single layer of noodles. Spoon about 1/3 of the remaining sauce over the noodles. Spoon about 1/2 of the ricotta cheese evenly over the layer of sauce, then sprinkle a few tablespoons of Parmesan cheese over that. Add about 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese.
  10. Repeat with another 1/3 of noodles, another 1/3 of the sauce, the remaining ricotta, another 1/3 of the mozzarella and a few tablespoons of Parmesan cheese.
  11. Finish with the last layer of noodles, the remaining sauce, Parmesan, and mozzarella cheese.
  12. Bake the lasagna, uncovered, for 45 minutes. If the top is browning too fast or the noodles are burning, cover loosely with aluminum foil.
  13. Remove from oven and let cool 10 minutes before slicing into squares and serving.
