Lasagna

Description

A perfect weeknight lasagna, that tastes just like home. This lasagna starts with a delectible homemade sauce that is incredibly easy to whip up. Along with the sauce, the fresh, full-moisture mozzarella brings a whole new level of flavor to an already flavorful dish.


Ingredients

For the Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef chuck (chuck or round)
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 cups water
    • <1i>(28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 1 bay leaf

For the Lasagna

  • 8 ounces lasagna noodles
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 16 ounces low-fat ricotta cheese
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 16 ounces mozzarella cheese (fresh, whole milk, sliced)
